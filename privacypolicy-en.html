Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. It is "Gypsy Cards" app policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Data Collection and Use

Our app collects and stores:

  • The card of the day selected and its date, stored locally on your device
  • Anonymous usage data for service analysis and improvement through Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Advertising identifiers to provide personalized ads through Google AdMob

Children's Data Protection

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years old ("Children"). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you know that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take the necessary measures.

Data Storage and Security

Card data is stored locally on your device using Android DataStore. Analytics and advertising data are securely processed and stored by Google services (Firebase and AdMob).

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may use advertising data such as:

  • Android Advertising ID (AAID)
  • App usage data
  • Approximate location data (if permitted)

For more information about how Google uses data, visit Google's Privacy Policy.

Advertising Partners

Our advertising partners (Google AdMob and its partners) may use tracking technologies to collect information about app usage and show personalized ads. You can visit the Google Play developer data policy for more information about how data is handled.

App Permissions

The "Gypsy Cards" app requires the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: required for displaying ads and collecting analytical data
  • Advertising Identifier: used for ad personalization

Data Sharing

We do not share personally identifiable information publicly or with third parties, except:

  • When required by law
  • For ad processing through Google AdMob
  • For usage analysis through Google Analytics for Firebase

Your Privacy Rights and Controls

  • You can disable ad personalization in your Android device settings under Settings > Google > Ads
  • You can request deletion of your analytical data by contacting us
  • You can opt out of sharing the advertising identifier in your device settings

Data Deletion

You can:

  • Clear app data in Android settings to remove local data
  • Request deletion of analytical data through our contact
  • Reset your advertising identifier in Android settings
